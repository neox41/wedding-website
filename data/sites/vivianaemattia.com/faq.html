What should I wear? Is there a dress code?

The dress code for our wedding is semi-formal/cocktail attire. Ladies should wear cocktail dresses, and the gentlemen should wear a suit and tie.

What will the weather be like this time of year?

It's warm but not too hot. It could be a bit chilly in the evening.

Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?

Both the ceremony and the wedding breakfast will be held indoor, however we have planned a few outdoor events (weather permitting).

What happens after the ceremony?

After the ceremony, the bridal party will be taking pictures nearby for around an hour. Guests can head straight to the reception hall where we will be serving finger food and beverages. A traditional Italian lunch will follow.

What should I do if I can’t make it?

Please let us know as soon as possible.

How do I get to the venue?

The venue is about 30-minutes drive from the church. Please note that Uber is not available in Italy and taxi services are very limited.
For those who have requested transportation, there will be a shuttle (already booked and paid) that will stop at the following stops:

  • At 12:30/13:00 - from the church to the restaurant
  • At 22:30 - from the restaurant, to Sesto FS (Via Monte Grappa, Sesto San Giovanni, 20099), Monza FS (Via Enrico Arosio, Monza, 20900) and the church (Via San Rocco 3, Monza, 20052)

What time should I arrive?

We recommend that you arrive 15/30 minutes before the start of the ceremony.

Where should guests park? Is the parking free?

There will be plenty of free parking space both at the church and at the venue

Where do you recommend I stay?

We would suggest you to stay in Monza or Milan, close to the church. Below some recommendations in Monza:

  • Hotel Royal Falcone
  • Al Giardino Segreto
  • Antica Trattoria Dell'Uva
  • Locanda San Paolo
  • Monza City Room and Studio
  • Euro Hotel Residence

When is the RSVP deadline? Do I need to mail back my RSVP or can I do it online?

Please confirm your attendance by 18th of April using the link we will send you. If you haven't received the link yet, please let us know.

Will food and drinks be served at the reception? What kind of food?

A full sit down Italian traditional meal will be served starting from 2pm (please be prepared to wait until that time :P).

Will there be any vegan / vegetarian / gluten free / special diet options?

Please let us know, along with your attendance confirmation, if you have any special dietary requirements, we will do our best to accommodate them.

What COVID-19 measures will you be taking during the event?

The wedding will be organised according to the Italian government guidance.

It is suggested to wear a face covering in the church. The restaurant does not require it.
